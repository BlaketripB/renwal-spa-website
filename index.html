Have Questions?

Your Questions, Answered

What does my first visit to the spa look like?
+
We want your first visit to feel seamless and stress-free. When you arrive, you will be warmly greeted and asked to complete a brief intake form covering your health history and any areas of focus. One of our therapists will then walk you through what to expect for your chosen service, answer any questions, and customize the session to your needs. Plan to arrive 10–15 minutes early so you can settle in, breathe, and begin to unwind before your treatment even starts.
What should I wear to the spa?
+
We recommend arriving in comfortable, loose-fitting clothing. For massage services, you will undress to your comfort level and be professionally draped with a sheet and blanket throughout your session — only the area being worked on is ever uncovered. For facial services, a headband and robe are provided. You are always in control of your comfort, and our therapists will check in with you throughout.
Can I bring my kids?
+
We lovingly ask that all guests be 18 years of age or older. Our space is intentionally designed as an adult sanctuary — a place to fully disconnect, decompress, and restore. We deeply appreciate your understanding and look forward to giving you the quiet, restorative experience you deserve.
What should I expect after a treatment?
+
It is completely normal to feel deeply relaxed, slightly drowsy, or even a little tender after your session — especially after deeper pressure work. We encourage you to drink plenty of water, avoid strenuous exercise for the rest of the day, and give your body time to integrate the work. Many clients notice continued benefits in the 24–48 hours following a treatment. The more consistently you come in, the more lasting the results.
